About Our Community

Microsoft Student Community SRMAP - Be a Force for Good!

The Microsoft Student Community, Amaravati at SRM University AP is a vibrant, collaborative student-led organization that brings together tech enthusiasts eager to explore, learn, and innovate with Microsoft tools and technologies. Our mission is to empower students with practical, hands-on experience in areas like Azure, AI, and cloud computing, fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment where members can develop real-world skills. Through workshops, hackathons, mentorship sessions, and industry speaker events, we aim to inspire future tech leaders and make a meaningful impact within the university and beyond.
